These are some of my original compositions, arranged chronologically.
  1. On The Way : This is my first OC, played entirely on one guitar, with a clean guitar tone. It's composed in the key of A Major, with a basic I-iii-ii-IV chord progression, giving a plagal cadence, with a catchy ostinato being the same baseline. The chords are major or minor sevenths, and sometimes ninths. It is played with some swing, giving a laid back feel.
